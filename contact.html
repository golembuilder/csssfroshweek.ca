About Us

The Computing Science Student Society is one of the largest and most active student societies at Simon Fraser University. We represent around 1400 students across three campuses and have a strong relationship with the School of Computing Science and the CS Co-operative Program.

The society holds multiple events every year ranging from simple socials and panel discussions, to workshops and hackathons. We are passionate about empowering students by supporting their personal and/or group projects through development workshops, hackathons, and panel discussions.

If you would like to know more about some of our initiatives, either as a sponsor and/or as a participant, please let us know.
